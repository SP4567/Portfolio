📚Research Column



Cryptanalysis Alongwith Intention Decoding in the Encrypted Corpus of Text Using Attention Transformer Mechanism

Cryptanalysis has gained significant attention recently, focusing on deciphering hidden messages and intentions within large text corpora. Deep learning models, particularly those based on attention transformer mechanisms, offer a promising approach that has yet to be explored in research. Traditional cryptanalysis techniques are becoming obsolete due to their vulnerabilities, while deep learning provides a layered and more secure alternative. This attention mechanism not only decodes text but also reveals the author's intent behind encrypted messages. This paper introduces a novel method for this purpose, which could significantly impact cybersecurity, digital forensics, and natural language processing by effectively decoding sentiments and intentions in encrypted communications.
